Note from the Election Commission of India

"The Election Commission of India is proud to introduce a secure and advanced e-voting platform to revolutionize the voting process in our country. This initiative aims to empower every eligible citizen to vote with ease and confidence, ensuring inclusivity, transparency, and modernization of democratic practices. By leveraging technology, we strive to make voting more accessible, secure, and engaging for all. Together, let us build a future where every voice is heard and every vote matters."

Why Vote with DeshGarv?

security

End-to-End Encryption

All votes are secured with SHA-256 cryptographic hashing and tamper-proof audit logs for complete election integrity.

face

AI Face Recognition

DeepFace-powered identity verification ensures only the registered voter can cast their ballot. Multiple faces are auto-flagged.

accessibility

Fully Accessible

Text-to-speech, high contrast mode, font scaling, and multi-language support for inclusive and barrier-free voting.

verified_user

Aadhaar + OTP Verification

Two-factor authentication using your Aadhaar number and a one-time password sent to your registered mobile number.
